Pasta al pesto

Description

This dish was born in Liguria that is a Northern Italy's region that overlooks the Tirreno sea. The name "pesto" in italian is mediated from the verb "pestare" that means "crushing". In fact the souse was originaly made crushing the ingrediants with a pestle.

Ingredients

  • Trofie (a fresh type of pasta)
  • Basil
  • Olive oil
  • Pecorino or Parmesan
  • Pine nuts
  • Salt

Steps

To prepare the pesto souse:

  1. Put the basil inside a bowl full of water with ice for 3 minutes.
  2. Mix the iced basil with olive oil, pine nuts, parmesan/pecorino and a bit of salt.
  3. Let it rest in the fridge for about 3/4 hours.

Finish the dish:

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Put the pesto souse on it.
  3. Enjoy.
