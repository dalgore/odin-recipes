Pizza

Description

Pizza is the most famous italian recipe. There are many versions, the most famous is the Naples's pizza, that is round with a soft and at the same time crispy frame.

Now we will prepare the most common variant that can be easly done at home with a regular oven.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato sause
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive oil
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Your favorite ingredients

Steps

  1. Mix water and flour with yeast and a bit of salt and let the dough rest for about 12 hours.
  2. Stretch the dough on a baking try.
  3. Cover it with tomato sause, mozzarella in pieces, basil and your favorite ingredients
  4. Bake it in the owen.
  5. Now you can enjoy your home made pizza.
