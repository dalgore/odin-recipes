Parmigiana

Description

Parmigiana is a typical italian dish, the name's origin is uncertain, but it's probably linked to the Parmesan cheese used to prepare it.

It's very simple to prapare, but it requires a bit of time to be done. The original recipe requires to fry the melanzana slices, but here we will prepare the lightest one version with rosted melanzana.

Ingredients

  • A big melanzana
  • Tomato sause
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Cut the melanzana in slices.
  2. Roast the slices until they become dry and brown on the surface.
  3. Dispose the first floor of slices in a little baking dish.
  4. Cover them with tomato sause, pieaces of mozzarella and grated Parmesan.
  5. Add a bit of salt and pieces of basil if you like it.
  6. Repeat the previous steps with another floor for at least two times.
  7. Now you can cook it in the oven.
